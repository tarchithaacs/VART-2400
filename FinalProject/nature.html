Theory of Nature

Callback and Conversation

Each swara holds significance. Each Raga has its own time of day, and emotion. Scientifically, each note is a pitch, and each frequency is a vibration. In Hindustani Classiscal Music, there is a deep connection between music and nature.

Each swara also is connected to nature in a unique way, often associated with an animal.

  • Sa - Peacock
  • Re - Bull/skylark
  • Ga - Goat
  • Ma - Dove/Heron
  • Pa - Cuckoo/nightingale
  • Dha - Horse
  • Ni - Elephant
